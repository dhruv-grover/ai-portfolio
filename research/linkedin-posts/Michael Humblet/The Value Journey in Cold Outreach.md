# The Value Journey in Cold Outreach

**Expert:** Michael Humblet  
**URL:** [LinkedIn Post URL](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/michaelhumblet_how-do-you-sell-not-by-shortcutting-not-activity-7371504965984473088-EsC6?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAADqypUsBkT9ygcGv9ZhtWV4kokXRCH2LsRo)

## Core Idea

Successful cold outreach is built on relevance and trust rather than immediately pitching a product. Buyers are more likely to engage when value is delivered progressively instead of leading with a sales pitch.

---

## The Value Journey

Michael introduces a three-step framework for increasing outbound effectiveness:

- **Inspire** – Capture attention by highlighting opportunities or challenges that are relevant to the buyer's role.
- **Educate** – Share insights that help the prospect better understand the problem and possible solutions, building credibility and trust.
- **Present the Solution** – Introduce the product or service only after establishing relevance and demonstrating value.

This sequence creates a more natural buying experience than leading with product features.

---



## Trust as the Competitive Advantage

As AI makes content creation and outreach easier, Michael argues that trust becomes the primary differentiator.

Organizations that consistently educate and provide relevant insights are more likely to earn buyer attention and improve conversion rates.

---



## Key Takeaways

- Cold outreach should prioritize relevance before pitching.
- Buyers engage more readily when value is delivered in stages.
- Inspiring and educating prospects builds trust before discussing the solution.
- Trust is becoming a key competitive advantage in AI-driven sales.
- A structured value journey can improve cold outreach conversion rates.
