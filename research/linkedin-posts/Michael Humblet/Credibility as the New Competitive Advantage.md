# Credibility as the New Competitive Advantage

**Expert:** Michael Humblet

## Core Idea

Modern B2B sales faces a credibility challenge rather than an attention challenge. While AI enables faster outreach, trust remains the deciding factor in whether buyers engage with sales teams.

---

## The Shift from Attention to Trust

Michael distinguishes the roles of marketing and sales by explaining that marketing captures attention, while sales earns trust.

Many organizations attempt to solve declining engagement by increasing outreach volume through automation. However, scaling messages without establishing credibility often reduces buyer confidence rather than improving it.

---



## Building Trust Before Outreach

With most buyers conducting independent research before engaging sales, credibility must be established before the first conversation.

Instead of competing only for buyers who are ready to purchase, sales teams should educate and provide valuable insights that position them as trusted advisors throughout the buying journey.

---



## The Future of B2B Sales

Michael argues that the highest-performing sales teams will be those that consistently build authority and trust before asking prospects for meetings. AI can accelerate execution, but long-term pipeline growth depends on credibility rather than outreach volume.

---



## Key Takeaways

- Trust has become the primary differentiator in modern B2B sales.
- AI increases outreach speed but cannot replace buyer confidence.
- Educational content helps establish credibility before direct engagement.
- Successful sales teams build relationships before requesting meetings.
- Long-term pipeline growth depends on trust, not outreach volume.
