## Core Idea

Modern B2B SaaS sales requires a combination of buyer-centric selling, AI adoption, consistent outbound execution, and personal brand building. Success comes from combining technology with trust rather than relying on traditional sales tactics alone.

---

## The 2026 Sales Playbook

Michael outlines five strategic priorities for modern sales teams:

- **Adopt a Buyer-Led GTM Approach** – Enable buyers with clarity and confidence instead of relying on aggressive selling techniques.
- **Leverage AI-Native Sales Platforms** – Use specialized AI tools to automate repetitive tasks and improve sales productivity.
- **Develop AI Management Skills** – Train sales teams to effectively collaborate with AI agents while maintaining human oversight.
- **Execute Daily Cold Email Outreach** – Build pipeline through consistent, targeted prospecting rather than infrequent large-scale campaigns.
- **Strengthen Personal Branding** – Establish credibility through thought leadership to build trust and generate inbound opportunities.

---



## Balancing AI with Human Expertise

Michael emphasizes that AI should enhance sales execution rather than replace human judgment. The greatest advantage comes from combining automation with authentic buyer relationships and industry expertise.

---



## Key Takeaways

- Modern GTM strategies should prioritize buyer enablement over product promotion.
- AI should improve efficiency while keeping humans responsible for strategic decisions.
- Consistent daily prospecting outperforms occasional large outreach campaigns.
- Personal branding has become an important driver of trust and pipeline generation.
- Sustainable sales growth comes from combining AI, disciplined execution, and buyer-focused selling.
