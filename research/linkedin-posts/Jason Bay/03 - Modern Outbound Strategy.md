## Core Idea

Traditional outbound activity alone is no longer enough.

Teams now succeed by combining excellent outbound execution with demand generation.

## Key Insights

### Path 1

Become exceptional at outbound.

- Better conversations
- Better conversion rates
- Better pipeline

### Path 2

Create demand.

Examples include:

- Thought leadership
- Webinars
- Events
- Podcasts
- Partnerships
- Executive networking
- Trigger-based prospecting

## Practical Takeaways

- Activity alone won't create pipeline.
- Brand and distribution create leverage.
- Marketing and sales should work together.

Explains the evolution of modern outbound strategy beyond traditional cold outreach.
