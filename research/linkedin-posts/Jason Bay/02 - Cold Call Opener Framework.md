## Core Idea

High-performing cold call openers follow a repeatable structure rather than relying on a script.

## Three-Part Framework

### Greeting

Answer the buyer's first question:
"Who are you?"

### Three Ts

- Trigger
- Tension
- Trust

At least one should be present.

### Permission

Ask permission to continue.

## Example Structure

Greeting → Trigger → Tension → Trust → Permission

## Practical Takeaways

- Avoid generic introductions.
- Use real buying signals.
- Establish credibility quickly.
- Ask for permission instead of launching into a pitch.
