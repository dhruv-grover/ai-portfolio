## Core Idea

Successful outbound is an organizational commitment, not just an SDR initiative.

## Requirements

- Leadership participation
- Front-line manager buy-in
- Shared methodology
- Clear qualification criteria
- Strong offers
- Problem-centric messaging
- High-quality data
- Continuous coaching
- Activity planning using sales math

## Key Takeaways

- Outbound transformation requires leadership.
- AI supports sales but doesn't replace execution.
- Consistent reinforcement matters more than one-time training.
