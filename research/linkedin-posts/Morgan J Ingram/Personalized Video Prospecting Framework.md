# Personalized Video Prospecting Framework

**Expert:** Morgan J. Ingram  
**URL:** [LinkedIn Post URL](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/morganjingramamp_one-rep-generated-450000-in-pipeline-after-activity-7455618130090868736-nJrc/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_android&rcm=ACoAADqypUsBkT9ygcGv9ZhtWV4kokXRCH2LsRo)

## Core Idea

Personalized prospecting videos remain one of the highest-performing outbound tactics because they demonstrate genuine effort and authenticity.

While many sales teams dismiss videos as "unscalable," consistent execution can generate significant pipeline with relatively little daily investment.

---



## The Math Behind Personalized Videos

Morgan shares the example of a sales rep who generated **$450,000 in pipeline** after implementing personalized LinkedIn videos.

The framework:

- 5 personalized videos per day
- Approximately 45 minutes of work
- Around 100 videos per month per rep

For a team of three reps:

- 300 personalized videos
- Conservative 5% meeting conversion rate
- Approximately 15 meetings booked
- Average deal size of $30,000
- Potential pipeline generated: **$450,000**

---



## Why Personalized Videos Work

Most buyers receive hundreds of AI-generated emails every week.

A personalized video immediately signals:

- A real human invested time researching them.
- Genuine effort beyond automated outreach.
- Higher credibility and authenticity.

The goal is not production quality—it is relevance.

---



## The 45-Second Video Framework

Morgan recommends keeping videos under **45 seconds**.

Include:

1. A specific observation about the prospect or company.
2. A relevant insight connected to your solution.
3. A simple call-to-action.
4. A friendly, natural delivery.

Avoid generic introductions or scripted pitches.

---



## Personalization Principles

Reference something meaningful, such as:

- Product launches
- Recent company announcements
- Career milestones
- Hiring activity
- Local events or city-specific observations

The more authentic the observation, the stronger the connection.

---



## The Competitive Advantage

Most sellers avoid personalized videos because they appear time-consuming.

Morgan argues this creates an opportunity:

> The activities that don't scale are often the ones competitors refuse to do.

Consistent execution compounds into meaningful pipeline over time.

---



## Key Takeaways

- Personalized videos outperform generic automated outreach.
- Five videos per day can create substantial pipeline over time.
- Keep videos under 45 seconds.
- Lead with genuine research and specific observations.
- Focus on authenticity rather than production quality.
- The willingness to do what others consider "unscalable" becomes a competitive advantage.
