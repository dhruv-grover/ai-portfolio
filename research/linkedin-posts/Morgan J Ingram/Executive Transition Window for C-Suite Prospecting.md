## Core Idea

One of the highest-converting outbound opportunities occurs when executives are in the **4–10 month transition window** at a new company. At this point, they have enough organizational context to recognize problems while still being motivated to make meaningful changes.

---



## Executive Transition Framework



### Step 1. Find New Executives

Use Sales Navigator to identify leaders who have recently joined a company.

Filter by:

- Less than one year at the company
- VP level and above

---



### Step 2. Target the Right Timing

Don't reach out immediately after the executive joins.

Instead, focus on executives who have been in their role for approximately **4–10 months**.

This is when they typically have:

- Better understanding of internal challenges
- Authority to drive change
- Greater urgency to improve results

Morgan refers to this as the **Executive Transition Window**.

---



### Step 3. Build a Saved Search

Save the Sales Navigator search so new prospects matching these criteria appear automatically every day.

This creates a repeatable pipeline instead of manually searching for prospects.

---



### Step 4. Connect First

Begin with a blank LinkedIn connection request.

According to Morgan, simple connection requests often achieve higher acceptance rates than heavily personalized ones.

---



### Step 5. Personalize Using Business Signals

After connecting, research recent company developments such as:

- Product launches
- Executive hires
- Office expansion
- Company announcements
- Organizational changes

Use these observations to create personalized outreach.

---



### Step 6. Deliver Observation-Based Outreach

Reach out with either:

- A personalized video under **40–50 seconds**
- A voice note
- A written message

The message should focus on a meaningful business observation rather than generic personalization.

---



## Why This Works

Executives in transition are actively evaluating their organization and are often looking for ways to improve performance.

Reaching them during this window increases the likelihood of meaningful conversations.

---



## Results

Using this framework:

- One AE team booked **5 C-suite meetings in 7 days**
- Generated approximately **$200,000 in qualified sales pipeline**

---



## Key Takeaways

- Executive transitions create high-intent outbound opportunities.
- The ideal outreach window is approximately **4–10 months** after an executive joins.
- Use Sales Navigator to automate prospect discovery through saved searches.
- Personalization should be based on observable business events rather than generic compliments.
- Observation-driven outreach helps break through the noise and generate executive conversations.
