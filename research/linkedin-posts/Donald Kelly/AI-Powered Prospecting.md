# AI-Powered Prospecting with Claude & Apollo MCP

**Expert:** Donald Kelly  
**URL:** [LinkedIn Post URL](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/donaldckelly_apolloanywhere-activity-7465344451092205568-y-Ll?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAADqypUsBkT9ygcGv9ZhtWV4kokXRCH2LsRo)

## Core Idea

AI should reduce time spent on prospect research and administrative work so sales reps can spend more time selling.

---

## AI-Enabled Prospecting Workflow

Donald demonstrates using Claude with Apollo.io's MCP connector to:

- Build targeted prospect lists.
- Identify decision-makers.
- Enrich contact data.
- Prepare prospects for outbound sequences.

This replaces hours of manual research with a streamlined AI-assisted workflow.

---



## Practical Application

The workflow can identify companies that match specific buying criteria, retrieve relevant contacts, and prepare them for outreach—all within a single AI workflow.

Rather than switching between multiple sales tools, prospecting becomes a continuous AI-assisted process.

---



## AI Supports the Sales Process

Donald emphasizes that AI is a productivity multiplier, not a replacement for outbound strategy.

Strong prospecting fundamentals still matter—AI simply removes repetitive operational work so reps can focus on customer conversations.

---



## Key Takeaways

- AI should automate research, not replace selling.
- AI workflows can significantly reduce manual prospecting effort.
- Centralizing research and enrichment improves outbound efficiency.
- Sales methodology remains more important than the technology itself.
- Effective AI adoption creates more selling time and stronger pipeline generation.
