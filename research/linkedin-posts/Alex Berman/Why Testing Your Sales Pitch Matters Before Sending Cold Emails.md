# Why Testing Your Sales Pitch Matters Before Sending Cold Emails

**Expert:** Alex Berman  
**URL:** [LinkedIn Post URL](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexanderberman_most-people-say-cold-email-is-dead-that-activity-7470847230057365505-shPD?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAADqypUsBkT9ygcGv9ZhtWV4kokXRCH2LsRo)

## Core Idea

Alex Berman argues that successful cold email campaigns are built long before the first email is sent. Rather than endlessly tweaking email copy, businesses should first validate their offer through real conversations, identify messaging that resonates with buyers, and then use those proven insights across every outbound channel.

---



## Validate Your Offer Through Real Conversations

Alex explains that many people give up on cold email after receiving few or no responses, assuming the channel no longer works. In reality, the issue often lies with an untested offer rather than the outreach method itself.

Before relying on cold email, Alex spent months pitching his services face-to-face at networking events. These conversations helped him discover which value propositions captured attention, which examples built credibility, and which messaging consistently sparked buyer interest.

---



## Let Buyer Feedback Shape Your Messaging

Repeated conversations reveal patterns that no amount of copywriting can uncover. Alex found that mentioning recognizable clients immediately changed how prospects reacted, leading to more meaningful conversations and stronger engagement.

Instead of guessing which words might work, he recommends paying close attention to the phrases that naturally encourage prospects to ask questions, qualify themselves, or express curiosity. Those insights become the foundation of high-performing outreach.

---



## Apply Proven Messaging Across Every Channel

Once messaging has been validated through real buyer interactions, it should be used consistently across cold emails, websites, LinkedIn content, and other marketing channels.

Alex emphasizes that skipping this validation process often leads companies to send thousands of ineffective cold emails without understanding why response rates remain low. Testing the pitch first creates a stronger foundation for scalable outbound campaigns and improves results across every customer touchpoint.

---



## Key Takeaways

- Cold email is more effective when the offer has already been validated through real conversations.
- In-person feedback helps identify messaging that genuinely resonates with buyers.
- Proven sales language should be applied consistently across email, LinkedIn, and websites.
- Buyer reactions provide more valuable insights than endlessly rewriting email copy.
- Strong outbound campaigns begin with a compelling offer, not just well-written emails.
