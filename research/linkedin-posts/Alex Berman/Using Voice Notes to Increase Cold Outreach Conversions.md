# Using Voice Notes to Increase Cold Outreach Conversions

**Expert:** Alex Berman  
**URL:** [LinkedIn Post URL](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexanderberman_i-had-a-guy-reply-to-one-of-my-cold-dms-activity-7402353177695551489-JKxG?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAADqypUsBkT9ygcGv9ZhtWV4kokXRCH2LsRo)

## Core Idea

Alex Berman argues that voice notes have become a powerful outbound sales tool because they create authenticity in a market saturated with AI-generated messages. When used strategically, voice notes help build trust, increase response rates, and accelerate deals without requiring lengthy sales calls.

---



## Why Voice Notes Stand Out

Alex explains that many prospects ignore long text responses because they resemble automated AI-generated messages. Instead, a short voice note immediately signals that a real person is behind the outreach, making buyers more likely to engage.

He shares an example where replacing a detailed text explanation with a brief voice note resulted in an immediate positive response, demonstrating how authenticity can improve conversion during outbound conversations.

---



## Best Practices for Using Voice Notes

Voice notes should be concise, ideally lasting less than 45 seconds, to maximize the likelihood that prospects will listen. Alex also stresses the importance of recording quality, noting that excessive background noise can undermine credibility and make messages difficult to understand.

He recommends using voice notes selectively for situations that benefit from additional context, such as explaining an offer, handling objections, or building trust. Simple requests, such as sharing a link or answering a short question, are better handled through text.

---



## Closing Deals Inside the Conversation

Alex encourages sales professionals to maintain momentum once a prospect shows buying intent. Rather than delaying the process by scheduling additional meetings, he suggests sharing payment links or simple agreements directly within the ongoing conversation whenever appropriate.

By combining authentic communication with a streamlined buying process, businesses can shorten sales cycles and close smaller B2B deals entirely through chat-based interactions.

---



## Key Takeaways

- Voice notes help outbound messages stand out by creating authenticity.
- Keeping voice notes under 45 seconds improves engagement.
- Clear audio quality is essential for maintaining credibility.
- Voice notes should complement, not replace, text-based communication.
- Maintaining momentum inside the conversation helps accelerate deal closure.
