# Other Research Materials

During the course of this research project, I reviewed additional podcasts, interviews, webinars, and company resources related to B2B SaaS cold outreach.

After reviewing this material, I chose not to include separate notes or transcripts in this directory because the key concepts and actionable insights were already captured through the selected LinkedIn posts and YouTube transcripts for each expert.

The goal of this repository was to avoid unnecessary duplication, keeping the knowledge base concise while preserving the information needed to generate a comprehensive Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS in a future iteration using LLM-assisted synthesis.
